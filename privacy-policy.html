COOKIES

The Website uses "cookies." A cookie is a small text file that is stored on the User’s computer for record-keeping purposes. A cookie does not identify the User personally or contain any other information about the User but it does identify the User’s computer.

The Company and some of its affiliates and third-party service providers may use a combination of “persistent cookies” (cookies that remain on the hard drive for an extended period of time) and “session ID cookies” (cookies that expire when the User closes their browser) on the Website to, for example, tracking overall site usage, and tracking and reporting on use and interaction with advertising impressions and advertisement services.

The Website uses a combination of cookies which serves various purposes and helps in functioning of the Website:

Strictly necessary cookies – these cookies are essential for the operation of the Website. Without the cookies, the Website cannot function, hence, the cookies are automatically accepted when a User visits the Website.

Functionality cookies – these cookies are enabled when a User visits the Website multiple times, for e.g., when the User exits the Website and re-enters it. These cookies support in storing information on the products and pages the User last visited, help in customizing search results and show relevant products to the Users.

If the User registers on the Website, these cookies will collect information on the User’s activities on the Website to understand which of the Products is of interest to the User.

Targeting cookies – these cookies record the User’s movement on the Website, the pages visited and the links which the User accessed. These cookies will also be used in social networking sites to share relevant Product information with the User if they have subscribed to the Company’s social media pages.

Analytical or performance cookies – The Website may use analytical cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to carry out analytics and enhance the Website. These cookies help the Company in analyzing the location of User visiting the Website, the pages which are most visited and how the User uses the Website. These cookies also help the Company in analyzing which Products are of maximum interest to the Users and if the Users are easily able to access them on the Website.

Users can set their browser to notify when cookies are received so that User will have an opportunity to either accept or reject it in each instance. However, User should note that refusing cookies may have a negative impact on the functionality and usability of the Website.
