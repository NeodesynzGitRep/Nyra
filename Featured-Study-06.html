What is energized water?

Energized water is water that has been infused with specific frequencies that align with the body’s natural vibrations, enhancing hydration and wellbeing. Nyra describes this as creating hexagonal clusters that improve cellular absorption.

How does Nyra energize water?

The Nyra app uses frequency patterns and sound vibrations delivered through a mobile device to charge water at a molecular level, using proprietary algorithms to restructure water for improved bioavailability.

Is this method safe?

Nyra positions the method as non-invasive, eco-friendly, and aligned to natural resonance patterns found in healthy biological systems.

What benefits can I expect?

Regular use may help improve cellular hydration, energy levels, emotional balance, immunity, and overall vitality, with users also reporting support for meditation, sleep, and physical performance.

Do I need any special equipment?

No. The experience is designed around just a mobile phone, the Nyra app, and clean drinking water.

How long does the charging process take?

Typical sessions range from 3 to 10 minutes depending on the selected wellness goal.
